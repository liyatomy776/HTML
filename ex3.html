About the College

Established with a vision to nurture engineering excellence in the heart of Kerala, **Amal Jyothi College of Engineering** is not just a campus—it’s a legacy in the making. We're the first engineering college in Kerala to receive **NAAC A-grade accreditation**, and we didn't stop there. With over **16.5 lakh sq. ft. of built-up academic brilliance**, a pass percentage that turns heads, and a student community driven by innovation and ethics, we proudly stand as one of India’s most trusted names in technical education.

Courses Offered

From next-gen **Computer Science and AI** to core **Civil and Mechanical engineering**, our B.Tech and M.Tech programs are built to power your future. With offerings that span MCA, MBA, and specialized master's programs, our curriculum blends theory with hands-on projects, giving students the industry relevance employers dream about.

Departments

Each department at Amal Jyothi is a powerhouse of research and learning. With specialized labs, experienced faculty, and a focus on student-led innovation, our departments—from CSE and EEE to Mechanical and Humanities—equip students not just to survive in the tech world, but to redefine it.

Facilities

Think beyond books. We offer **state-of-the-art labs**, a vibrant campus WiFi ecosystem with **2.04 Gbps speed**, massive hostels cared for by Catholic priests and sisters, modern cafeterias, and sports complexes that let students recharge the right way. If you’re looking for a college that *feels* like a campus and a community, this is it.
